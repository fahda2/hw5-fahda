# Saudi Public Holiday Reference Notes

## Saudi weekend
Friday and Saturday are the official weekend in Saudi Arabia (since 2013).
Sunday–Thursday are working days.

## Official public holidays (annually recurring)
| Holiday | Approximate Gregorian Window |
|---|---|
| Founding Day | February 22 |
| Saudi National Day | September 23 |
| Eid al-Fitr | ~3–4 days, shifts ~11 days earlier each Gregorian year |
| Eid al-Adha | ~4 days (incl. Arafat Day), shifts ~11 days earlier each year |

## Moon-sighting note
Eid al-Fitr and Eid al-Adha start dates are announced by the Saudi Supreme
Court based on moon sighting. Projected dates in the script may be off by
±1 day. The script marks these with `# ~` in the holiday dictionary.
